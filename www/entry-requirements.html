Entry Requirements

  • Citizens and Residents
    • Must be vaccinated
    • Negative PCR test valid for no more than 96 hours and conducted in the UAE.
    • Green pass on Al Hosn App.
    • EDE screening will be done at all entry points in the venue.
  • International participants who are not vaccinated
    • Present a negative PCR test valid for no more than 48 hours and conducted in the UAE.
    • Present their passport and visa as well at the entrance.
    • EDE screening will be done at all entry points in the venue.
  • International participants who are vaccinated 
    • Present a negative PCR test valid for no more than 96 hours and conducted in the UAE.
    • A vaccination certificate
    • EDE screening will be done at all entry points in the venue.

Event attendance is restricted to vaccinated individuals only.

Participants need to
present a negative PCR test result taken within 96 hours prior entrance to the event

Event participants should present AlHosn App green pass

Participants must provide that no more than 6 months have passed since the second dose and 3 months have passed for the most vulnerable groups of the elderly and those with chronic disease
EDE screening will be done at all entry points in the venue.

Participants should be mindful of

Physical distancing
Mandatory wearing of a face mask
Regular sanitisation of their hands

  Events’ attendance is granted for both vaccinated & nonvaccinated individuals.

Before booking your trip to attend an event in Abu Dhabi, check the status of your country on the Visit Abu Dhabi Website.
Requirements for participants arriving from a Green Country. The below steps is for travelers to register their vaccination.
  1. Register your arrival by logging into ICA.gov.ae when you plan your trip
  2. Complete the registration arrival form
  3. Before boarding to Abu Dhabi, both vaccinated & unvaccinated travellers, must present a negative PCR test result from within 48 hours of departure
  4. Download AlHosn app and register your account using the UID that you received earlier and your phone number
  5. You will receive an OTP to complete the registration on AlHosn App
Vaccinated visitors
  • PCR test is required on arrival and on day 6, if staying in Abu Dhabi
  • No quarantine is required
  • Present a negative PCR test result taken within 96 hours prior entrance to the event
Non-vaccinated visitors
(will not be allowed to enter the event)
  • PCR test is required on arrival and on day 6 & day 9, if staying in Abu Dhabi
  • No quarantine is required
  • Present a negative PCR test result taken within 48 hours prior entrance to the event
Fully vaccinated individuals with over six months from their second dose must have a booster shot to retain their green status on the Al Hosn App. A green status is required for fully vaccinated individuals to enter the emirate from within the UAE. Unvaccinated individuals are required to present a negative PCR test result received within 96 hours. EDE scanning will also be required at the land border to rapidly detect potential COVID-19 infections.
For more details on the green pass, please visit this link.
