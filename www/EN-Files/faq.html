FAQ

Thank you for visiting our FAQ page. Here you will find the answers to our most frequently asked questions.

What is IDC Conference?

The International Defence Conference (IDC) is a merged and consolidated of two signature conferences, namely; Abu Dhabi International Offset Conference (ADIOC) which was managed and organized by Tawazun Economic Council and IDEX Conference managed by Abu Dhabi National Exhibitions Company in cooperation with the Ministry of Defence and the General Command of the UAE Armed Forces. The theme of IDC 2021 Conference is “The Prosperity and Development of Artificial Intelligence and Advanced Technology and the Protection in the Era of the 4IR”.

Where does IDC take place?

The event will take place at ADNOC Business Centre, Abu Dhabi, UAE. The event will be live streamed and attendees can view it virtually as well. both formats will be attended by invitations only.

When does IDC take place?

Saturday 20th February 2021 08:00-14:00 (UAE Time Zone – GMT+4)

Is there a registration Fee to attend the International Defence Conference?

There is no registration fee. The in-person event is by invitation only, and the virtual event can be accessed online if you receive an online invitation.

Can I access the conference with my IDEX exhibition badge?

You can only access the in-person IDC Conference if you hold an IDC invitation in addition to the IDEX badge. IDEX exhibition badge does not get you access. However, if you are registered with IDEX and NAVDEX newsletter and database, you will receive an e-invitation to the virtual broadcast of the conference.

Where can I view the most current agenda?

The latest agenda and confirmed speakers is available via the IDC website, which will be uploaded soon.

Will there be free Wi-Fi at the conference venue?

Yes, the venue has free Wi-Fi during the conference dates
